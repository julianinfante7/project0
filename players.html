Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo was born in Portugal 35 years ago and actually plays in the italian league (Serie A) for a club named Juventus. Throughout his career he has won dozens of individual and team thropies.

Lionel Messi

Messi was born in Argentina 32 years ago and he plays in the spanish league (Liga Santander) for F.C Barcelona since the year 2000. He is considered by the majority of the soccer fans as the best player in the history of soccer.

Neymar

Neymar is a 28 year old brazilian player that plays in the french league (Ligue 1) for a club named PSG. He is known for his outstanding playing style, known by the world of soccer as freestyle.

Mbappe

Mbappe is a 21 year old french player that is Neymar's teammate in PSG. Even though he is very young, he has won prestigious trophies along his career, such as the World Cup.
