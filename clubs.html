Best Soccer Competitions
Real Madrid

Real Madrid C.F, founded in 1902, is the greatest soccer club in soccer history. This club has won 13 times the Champions League and 33 times La Liga Santander.

Barcelona

F.C Barcelona, founded in 1899, is one of the best soccer clubs in the word. This club owns multiple trophies and have received great players like: Lionel Messi, Ronaldinho, Iniesta, etc.

Liverpool

Liverpool F.C was founded in 1892 and is one of the best english soccer teams in the history of the sport. They are the former Champions League champions.
